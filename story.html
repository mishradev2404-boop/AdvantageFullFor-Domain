Born from within LinkedIn’s own walls, AdVantage was founded to bring insider expertise to the B2B world’s most misunderstood advertising platform.

The Insider's Epiphany

Our founding team spent years on the inside of LinkedIn — working directly within the platform's ecosystem. That gave us a front-row seat to the advertising efforts of thousands of companies across every imaginable industry.

We noticed a recurring, painful pattern: brilliant B2B brands were burning budget. They were using Google Search tactics or Meta "interest" targeting on a platform built for professional identity. They were chasing vanity clicks while decision-makers scrolled right past them.

We realized that the most powerful features of LinkedIn Ads—internal audience insights, emerging ad formats, and specific algorithm updates—weren't being utilized because they aren't always shared publicly. Most agencies were operating on outdated playbooks. And even fewer understood how paid campaigns, organic content, and thought leadership work together to build true market authority.

So, we left the mothership to build the agency we knew was needed. One that speaks "LinkedIn" as a native language and understands the entire ecosystem—from ads to content to community.
