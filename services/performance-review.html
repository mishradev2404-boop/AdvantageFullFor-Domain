Common questions

Is the review free?

The initial audit is offered as a free, no-obligation review. There is no requirement to work with us afterwards.

What access do you need?

Read access to your LinkedIn Campaign Manager account is enough to begin. Access to your CRM or analytics makes the lead-quality analysis considerably more useful.

How long does it take?

Most reviews are turned around within a few business days, depending on account size and how quickly access is granted.

Will you just tell us to spend more?

No. A common outcome is that spend should be consolidated rather than increased — a smaller budget spent well usually beats a larger one spread thin.
